Message From the President

It gives me great pleasure to welcome you to the Red Sea University (RSU). RSU is the right place to be in your search of quality higher education. Recovering from the civil war and destruction the education infrastructure of our country Red Sea University was established and officially opened 2008 in Bosaso north east in Somalia Puntland State, the concept of the Establishment Red Sea University was building community by exploring problems of society, producing young educated generations and utilizing our country’s resources in the best manner by conducting research, teaching and learning.

Prof. Mohamed Osman Nuh
